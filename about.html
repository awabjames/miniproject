We're reptile breeders currently based in Zamboanga City and started our journey last 2018. At first, our goal was to collect quality ball python morphs from big time breeders based in NCR.
Now that our collection grew, we also want to provide quality balls at a reasonable price. We are not here to compete with other breeders who have established their reputation in this hobby but we simply want to work with them to provide quality morphs to the growing reptile community.
We have already obtained our wildlife farm permit (WFP) issued by DENR Region IX so all the reptiles coming from our farm are with legal papers. We want our clients to have easy access to our available reptiles in just few clicks away. We hope for you to have a good one and as what they all say "happy keeping!"
