GUARANTEE

We guarantee all our reptiles to be healthy, established feeders and correctly sexed before shipping. I'm providing live guarantee during the transport and 14 days (guarantee window) after the client receives the reptile/s. I will refund or replace (same morph/gender if available) if it dies during the guarantee window only if the client can provide a written proof from Veterinarian that it died of natural causes and not due to mistreatment.
